2. Context-specific Function Prediciton Results

We assigned 1126 functions to 895 genes, 779 known and 116 unknown, with a validation accuracy of 70%. Note that predictions on known genes were served only for validation in previous studies. Our predictions on known genes, on the other hand, are of great use in practice because they provide the context information(network topology) of genes' function. Among our assignment, 20% genes are assigned with multiple functions upon different network environments. All the prediction results are in this file. If the is_correct column is -1, the prediction is for an unknown gene. If it's 0, the prediction is wrong for a known gene. If it's 1, the prediction is correct for a known gene.
